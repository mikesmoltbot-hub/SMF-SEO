# SMF Works Brand Voice Guide

## Core Principles

1. **Direct** — Say what you mean. No beating around the bush.
2. **Practical** — Every piece of advice should be actionable.
3. **No Fluff** — Cut the corporate speak. "Leverage" and "synergize" are banned.
4. **Show, Don't Tell** — Use examples, not adjectives.

## Tone Examples

### ❌ Don't Write Like This

"In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, businesses must leverage cutting-edge AI technologies to remain competitive and drive operational efficiencies."

### ✅ Write Like This

"AI can handle the repetitive stuff so you can focus on growing your business. Here's how to set it up in 10 minutes."

## Headline Formulas

- "How to [Achieve Result] Without [Common Pain Point]"
- "The [Number]-Minute [Task] That [Benefit]"
- "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong (And What To Do Instead)"
- "[Number] Ways to [Result] (That Actually Work)"

## CTA Examples

- "Try it yourself."
- "Here's the code."
- "Questions? Ask."
- "Ready when you are."

## Words to Avoid

- Leverage
- Synergy
- Holistic
- Scalable (unless technical context)
- Disruptive
- Innovative (show, don't claim)
- Best-in-class
- World-class

## Words to Use Instead

- Use (not leverage)
- Together (not synergy)
- Complete (not holistic)
- Grows with you (not scalable)
- Actually works (not innovative)
