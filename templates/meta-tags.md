# Meta Tags Template

## Title Tag Formulas

### For Listicles
"[Number] Best [Topic] for [Audience] in [Year] | SMF Works"
Example: "10 Best AI Tools for Small Business in 2026 | SMF Works"

### For How-To Guides
"How to [Achieve Result]: [Qualifier] | SMF Works"
Example: "How to Automate Lead Gen: A Step-by-Step Guide | SMF Works"

### For Comparison Posts
"[Option A] vs [Option B]: Which is Better for [Use Case]? | SMF Works"
Example: "Zapier vs Make: Which is Better for Small Business? | SMF Works"

### For Ultimate Guides
"The Complete Guide to [Topic] ([Year]) | SMF Works"
Example: "The Complete Guide to AI Automation (2026) | SMF Works"

## Meta Description Formulas

### Formula 1: Problem + Solution + CTA
"Struggling with [problem]? Learn how to [solution] with this [content type]. [CTA]."

### Formula 2: Benefit + Proof + CTA
"Discover how to [benefit]. Based on [proof/data]. [CTA]."

### Formula 3: Question + Answer + CTA
"[Question]? [Brief answer]. Read on to learn [what they'll discover]. [CTA]."

## Examples

**Title:** 10 Best AI Tools for Small Business in 2026 | SMF Works
**Meta:** Discover the best AI tools for small business. We tested 47 options and ranked the top 10 by ease of use and ROI. See which tool fits your budget.

**Title:** How to Automate Lead Gen: A Step-by-Step Guide | SMF Works
**Meta:** Struggling to generate leads consistently? Learn how to build an automated lead gen system that runs 24/7. Includes templates and setup instructions.

**Title:** Zapier vs Make: Which is Better for Small Business? | SMF Works
**Meta:** Can't decide between Zapier and Make? We compared pricing, features, and ease of use. See which automation platform wins for small business.

## Character Limits

- **Title:** 50-60 characters (Google truncates at ~580px)
- **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters

## Tips

- Include target keyword near the front of title
- Use active voice
- Include a clear benefit
- Add urgency or specificity when possible
- End with a subtle CTA
